Welcome to Grand Villa MHC

Grand Villa MHC is a premier age-restricted 55+ senior community located in Springfield, MO 65802. With 69 well-maintained sites, we offer "Experience the Best of Senior Living in the Heart of the Ozarks." Our community provides senior-focused programming, social activities, and easy-walk paved streets with an emphasis on affordable ownership versus renting. Located in Springfield's regional hub with strong healthcare access including CoxHealth and Mercy, residents enjoy below-average cost of living while having access to robust amenities and retail for a midsize city.
